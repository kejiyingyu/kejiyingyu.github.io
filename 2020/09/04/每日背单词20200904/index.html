每日背单词20200904

feud 世仇

No stranger to headlines, the company and its biggest investor have been caught in a long-running feud over the future of the company.

经常登上头条新闻的这家公司及其最大投资者已经陷入了一场关于公司未来的长期争斗中。

mammoth 巨大

Governments face mammoth logistical challenge to deliver COVID-19 vaccine.

各国政府在运送新冠肺炎疫苗方面面临巨大的后勤挑战。

evermore 日益

As a world leader in logistics, we want to share our experience of operating during one of the biggest health crises in recent history, in order to develop strategies in an evermore connected world.

作为全球物流领域领导者，我们希望将这次近代史上最大的健康危机之一中得到的运营经验分享给大家，以便在一个日益互联的世界中制定战略部署。

leapfrog 跨越

efficacy 功效

As COVID-19 vaccines have leapfrogged development phases, the lady said stringent temperature requirements are likely to be imposed for certain vaccines to ensure their efficacy is maintained during transportation and warehousing.

随着新冠肺炎疫苗已经经过开发阶段，这位女士表示，可能会对某些疫苗实施严格的温度要求，以确保在运输和仓储过程中保持其功效。

kickstart 开始

The lady hopes the study will help kickstart the dialogue among the different actors and improve pandemic resilience in medical supply logistics.

这位女士希望这项研究将有助于开启不同参与者之间的对话，并提高医疗供应物流中抵御流行病的能力。
