每日背单词20200902

每日背单词20200902

name and shame 点名羞辱

The bill is designed to name and shame big businesses with a total income of more than $100m a year into paying their smaller suppliers within 30 days or less.

该法案旨在点名要求年收入超过1亿美元的大企业在30天以内向规模较小的供应商付款。

amass 积累

The Group is understood to have been in the market on Monday night in an effort to amass more stock in the residential developer.

据了解，该集团周一晚间已在市场上买入，试图积累更多该住宅开发商的股票。

gaining a foothold站稳脚跟

However, it has been unsuccessful in gaining a foothold in the real estate business.

然而，它在房地产业务中没有成功站稳脚跟。

remit 委托权限；职权范围；提交审议的事项

The Group has a $181m market value and is broadening its remit to alternative assets.

该集团市值为1.81亿美元，正在将其业务范围拓展至替代资产。
